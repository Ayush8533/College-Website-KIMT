About Krishna Group

Where dreams are born, inspirations are given and achievements are made

Krishna Institute of Management and Technology (KIMT), established in the year 2020, under the aegis of Krishna Welfare Educational Society. The KWES is a registered, non-government, non-profit educational organization engaged in the field of training, research and consultancy directed towards the field of Management, Computer Applications, Para Medical Science, Engineering, Science, Art, Commerce, Vocational Education, Home Science and Basic Education.

The College has a wide campus with lush green and well maintained ground, state of the art infrastructure, modern laboratories for various programs, a well rich library, well developed sport facilities along with an ambience suitable for absolute learning. More than 100+ Courses are available in various branches.

The College is located at Sona within 25 KM on the Aonla – Sirauli Road, Vishanpuri Bagiya Churaha. The college is well connected to the Ahikshetra Parshwanath Bhagwan, Jain Digambar Mandir (अहिछत्र पार्श्वनाथ भगवान, जैन श्वेतांबर मंदिर रामनगर आंवला) transversely linked between Bareilly - Aonla – Moradabad - Delhi.
